who we are

Sweetspire Studio is a small graphic design studio working in brand identity, print, and editorial. The studio is led by a single designer who handles every project from first sketch to final delivery — no handoffs, no committee, no diluted intent.

We're drawn to work that rewards a second look. Identities that feel like they were always there. Print pieces with a little weight in the hand. Layouts that breathe. Type that knows when to be quiet.

what we do

  • Brand identity — logomarks, wordmarks, color systems, type pairings, and the small rules that hold a brand together.
  • Print & editorial — books, zines, lookbooks, programs, posters, and the kind of paper-craft work that benefits from a careful eye.
  • Packaging — labels, wraps, boxes, and tactile systems for products that want to feel like a small gift.
  • Art direction — collaborations with photographers, illustrators, and writers to give a campaign or publication a shared visual logic.

how we work

Slowly, mostly. We take on a small number of projects at a time so each gets the attention it deserves. Every engagement starts with a conversation — about the work, the people it's for, and the feeling you want it to leave behind. From there, we make.

If any of this sounds like the kind of company you'd like to keep, we'd love to hear from you.

about

Sweetspire Studio is a small creative studio working in graphic design and illustration. The studio is led by a single designer who handles every project from first sketch to final delivery — no handoffs, no committee, no diluted intent.

My work lives at the intersection of common stories and the natural world. I'm drawn to expressive, uplifting visual work that speaks to everyday people and the places we share with everything else that lives here. I try to bring modern design conventions back to their emotional roots through tactile, nostalgic imagery and symbolism — the kind of work that rewards a second look.

how I work

Slowly, mostly. I take on a small number of projects at a time so each gets the attention it deserves. Every engagement starts with a conversation — about the work, the people it's for, and the feeling you want it to leave behind. From there, we make.

let's talk

If this sounds like the kind of company you'd like to keep, I'd love to hear from you. The contact page has everything you need to get in touch.
